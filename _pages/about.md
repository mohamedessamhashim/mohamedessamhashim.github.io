I am a Physician-Researcher (M.B.,B.S./MD) with a passion for leveraging artificial intelligence to solve complex scientific challenges, particularly in neuroscience-related AI domains.

My research spans across advanced medical imaging and statistical modeling. As a Medical AI Research Collaborator at the Kann Lab (MGB/Harvard Medical School), I develop LLM-assisted segmentation pipelines for pediatric gliomas (BraTS-PEDs).

My technical toolkit includes Python (PyTorch, MONAI, HuggingFace), R, and deep learning architectures (nnU-Net, Swin-UNETR), coupled with strong clinical foundations such as NIHSS certification and direct patient care experience in diverse environments across Egypt, Tanzania, and Sudan.

Please feel free to explore my portfolio and publications, or reach out to connect!
