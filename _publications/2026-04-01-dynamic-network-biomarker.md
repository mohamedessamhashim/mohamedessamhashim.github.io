This research explores the application of Dynamic Network Biomarker (DNB) theory to Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) proteomics. By analyzing longitudinal data, we successfully identified a critical transition module—a specific subset of proteins acting as early-warning signals for the irreversible progression of Alzheimer's disease. This provides potential targets for early intervention before significant cognitive decline occurs.
