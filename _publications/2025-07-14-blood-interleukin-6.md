This study investigated the association between IL-6 mRNA expression and Alzheimer’s Disease (AD), Mild Cognitive Impairment (MCI), and Control (CTL) groups. By analyzing blood transcriptome data from the GEO Database for 329 participants, we applied a General Linear Model (GLM) controlling for age and gender covariates to explore systemic inflammatory markers in neurodegeneration.
