This graduation thesis presents a comprehensive evaluation of the Awareness, Attitudes, and Practices (KAP) among medical students at the University of Medical Sciences & Technology (UMST) regarding infection prevention during the COVID-19 pandemic. The findings highlight areas for targeted educational interventions to improve clinical safety compliance among future healthcare providers.
