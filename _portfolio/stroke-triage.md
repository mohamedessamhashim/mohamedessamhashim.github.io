Developed a full-stack Django-based web application tailored for emergency stroke triage environments.

- Implemented standard clinical scoring systems, including NIHSS, BE-FAST+, RACE, and ASPECTS.
- Integrated AHA/ASA guideline recommendations to support rapid, data-driven decisions for tPA administration and mechanical thrombectomy.
- Ensured a streamlined, clinical-friendly user interface.
